By the numbers

Potential ROI and risk.

These are the main Phuket investment pathways. ROI is indicative, not guaranteed. We show real friction so you can decide with eyes open.

Build new

20–35% ROI
18–36 months · Medium–High risk

You own the land and the finished asset. Highest upside, longest timeline.

  • Title-transfer delays
  • Planning-permit amendments
  • Contractor scope creep
  • Material inflation
  • Liquidity tied up during build

Off-plan

15–25% ROI
12–24 months · Medium–High risk

Strong entry price at spec stage. You customize finishes with us after purchase.

  • Developer delays
  • Spec-price changes
  • Finish-quality variance
  • Resale-market timing at handover
  • Foreign-ownership quota limits

Value-add

15–30% ROI
12–24 months · Medium–High risk

Buy discounted resale, renovate, then sell, rent out, or move in.

  • Hidden structural costs
  • Permit frictions for renovations
  • Leasehold/transfer complications
  • Rental-management gaps
  • Resale absorption timing
